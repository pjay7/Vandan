About Us

We are a family-run business delivering 100% organic and pure groundnut oil since 2010.

Our mission is to bring natural oil extraction methods to modern kitchens with purity, tradition, and sustainability. We extract oil from premium-quality peanuts directly sourced from Junagadh, Gujarat. Our oil is completely chemical-free and contains zero preservatives, making it ideal for health-conscious households.

We proudly operate from Mehsana, Gujarat, delivering trusted quality across North Gujarat through verified pickup points and home delivery (within Mehsana city only).
